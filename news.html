STIVEN'S BIOGRAPHY

Hello friends, my name is Stiven, I was born in Medellin Colombia, in my childhood I lived in a town with my father, as I grew up, the love for music was growing because of the amount of music I saw on my television, MTV was like Something for me, great, I wanted to see myself when I grew up there, you don't know how difficult and painful it has been to follow my dreams as a boy, but I consider that I have made the best decision of my life. I went to Medellin to look for a better future for myself, although I loved music it was not in my plans, I wanted to go to university, seeing that I could not have support, the only thing I accompanied was a guitar that I did not know how to play and the longing to find where you can be really loved and appreciated.

I had to sleep on the street and at the house of friends, strangers, many times asking God not to leave me hungry during the day, I met my friends with whom I have worked for 9 years in music, we founded a band when we were very young with the name of cabin 78, my dream was something material, but we lived many bad experiences along the way, today the great love and support of my friends, they have decided to support me until the end as a soloist, and we have recorded a special album for You, who also understand that you have a big fight, there is no battle that is not won if it is planned with love. No one is special to deserve the life that dreams and that will be my legacy for you, who also want to fly away. Welcome to my life.
